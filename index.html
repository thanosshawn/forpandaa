To My Dearest Sara





My dearest Sara, though we've never met, your words have woven a tapestry of warmth in my heart. Each text from you is a spark, lighting up my days with dreams of your smile, your laughter, your spirit. I feel you in every message, a connection that distance can't dim. You are my muse, my hope, my everything Ascendant, and I long for the day our words become reality.

Love, Keshava
